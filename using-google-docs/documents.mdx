## Create and Export to PDF in Google Docs

This article guides you through creating a new document in Google Docs and exporting it as a PDF file.

### Steps

1. Open Google Docs.

2. Click the "+" icon to create a new document.

3. Name your document by clicking on "Untitled document" in the upper left corner and entering a name.

4. Add content to your document.

5. Click "File" in the top menu.

6. Select "Download" and then choose "PDF Document (.pdf)".

7. Your document will now be downloaded as a PDF file.
